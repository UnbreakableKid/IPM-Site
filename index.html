Hungry Readers: Group 2 - Lab Class 1

We are a group of students who got together to carry out a project given in the IPM course. This project consists in a web application where readers of every kind can discuss, trade and discover books with other fellow readers. By promoting interaction not only among readers, but also among authors, we hope to encourage people to share their favorite books with others, as well as to take recommendations from their peers.

Because reading doesn't have to be a lonely hobby.
